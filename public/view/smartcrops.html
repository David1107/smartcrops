IRRIGATION

  • IRRIGATION CROP

    In this option you would irrigate the whole crop. Hence, the tomato, coriander and arugula will be irrigated.

  • IRRIGATION TOMATO

    In this option you would irrigate just the tomato. In this case, the irrigation will be for 10 seconds.

  • IRRIGATION CORIANDER

    In this option you would irrigate just the coriander. In this case, the irrigation will be for 7 seconds.

  • IRRIGATION ARUGULA

    In this option you would irrigate just the arugula. In this case, the irrigation will be for 8 seconds.
